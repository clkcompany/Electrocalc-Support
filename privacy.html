Privacy Policy

ElectroCalc · Last updated: May 22, 2026

OVERVIEW

Our Commitment to Privacy

ElectroCalc is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or share your personal data. All calculations and saved results remain entirely on your device.

Information We Do Not Collect

ElectroCalc does not collect:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Device identifiers
  • Calculation inputs or results
  • Photos or images captured via the Resistor Scanner

Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device only using iOS secure storage (UserDefaults / SharedPreferences). It is never transmitted to any server:

  • Saved calculation results
  • Saved circuit simulator designs
  • App preferences and settings
  • Pro upgrade status

Camera Permission

The Resistor Color Code Scanner feature requests access to your camera. Camera access is used solely in real time to identify resistor color bands. No images or video are saved, uploaded, or transmitted anywhere.

In-App Purchases

ElectroCalc offers a one-time "Pro" upgrade through Apple's App Store in-app purchase system. All payment processing is handled exclusively by Apple. We do not receive or store any payment information. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details about purchase data.

Third-Party Services

ElectroCalc does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. The app does not contain ads.

Children's Privacy

ElectroCalc does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since we collect no personal data from any user, the app is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
